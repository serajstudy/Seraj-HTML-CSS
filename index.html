Welcome to Motor Show Room

A car dealership, or car dealer, is a business that sells
new or used cars, at the retail level, based on a dealership
contract with an automaker or its sales subsidiary. Car dealerships
also often sell spare parts and automotive maintenance services.A
motor vehicle showroom is a retail space where vehicles, typically cars,
are displayed for sale to potential customers. It's a key part of an automotiv
dealership and offers customers a hands-on experience with the vehicles before making a purchase.
